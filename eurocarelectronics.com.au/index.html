European car upgrades, tuning & Electronic Repairs specialists


  • About euro car electronics

Euro Car Electronics specialises in circuit board diagnostics and repair of precise electronic equipment in the Sunshine Coast area.

We specialise in diagnostics & repair of valuable modules in luxury vehicles & boats as well as agricultural & construction equipment. This is helping our clients save costs on replacement modules, time required to order these in and it helps the environment by reducing electronic waste. Euro Car Electronics also specialises in performance upgrades to maximise your vehicle potential as well as save the fuel by fine-tuning & software engineering vehicles’ Engine Management Systems. We also tune Trucks, Agricultural equipment & fleets of vehicles to help company owners save big on running costs & increase the safety of their drivers with speed limit settings and others.

Euro Car Electronics is interested in collaboration with businesses who want to save costs on electronic maintenance as well as running costs of their fleets.
